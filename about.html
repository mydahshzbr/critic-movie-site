A space for the quiet observer.

critic. was built for those who find meaning in the credits, the score, and the way a film stays with you long after the screen goes black.

The Philosophy

In an age of endless scrolling, we wanted a place to pause. critic. isn't about being a professional reviewer—it's about keeping a personal map of your cinematic journey.

The Features

Track what you watch, save what you love, and build a diary that grows as your tastes evolve. No ads, no social pressure—just you and the movies.
